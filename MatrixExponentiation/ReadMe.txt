

Given a matrix A, and an integer N, Calculate A^N

Normal time complexity = O(M^3 * N)
Optimized time complexity = O(M^3 * logN)



Link -> https://www.spoj.com/problems/MPOW/
Q. MPOW - Power of matrix

You will be given a square matrix M and a positive integer power N. 
You will have to compute M raised to the power N. (that is, M multiplied with itself N times.)
